Frequently Asked Questions

How does this system help me as a farmer? ▼

Our project helps monitor soil carbon emissions using IoT sensors (MQ135). It predicts emission levels and suggests ways to improve soil health.

How does the system detect carbon emissions in my soil? ▼

We use ESP32 with MQ135 sensors to measure air quality near the soil. The data is processed using Random Forest Regression to predict carbon levels.

What actions can I take to reduce soil carbon emissions? ▼

You can: ✅ Use organic fertilizers ✅ Avoid over-tilling to reduce soil disturbance ✅ Implement carbon sequestration techniques like cover cropping

How frequently should I check the emission data? ▼

It’s best to check weekly or after major farming activities like plowing or fertilization The dashboard updates in real time!
